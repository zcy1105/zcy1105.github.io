About Me

I am currently pursuing a Master of Engineering in Financial Engineering at Cornell University, with an expected graduation date of December 2024. I completed my Bachelor of Science in Mathematics with honors at the University of Washington in June 2023, and I also participated in an exchange program at Tsinghua University in Beijing, China, where I studied Mathematics. My academic interests include Optimization Modeling in Finance, Monte Carlo Simulation, Derivatives Securities, Investment and Portfolio Optimization, Stochastic Process, Operations Research Tools for Finance, Statistics for Data Science, and Machine Learning.
